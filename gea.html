Global Environmental Agencies

Like us, some of these organizations may be a charity, a trust, a non-governmental organization.

Some environmental issues they focus on include pollution, waste, and climate change.

If you want to get more information about these agencies, you can click on the links provided below.

“The Earth is a fine place and worth fighting for.”
Ernest Hemingway, Writer

Rainforest Trust is a US-based nonprofit environmental organization focused on the purchase and protection of tropical lands to strategically conserve threatened species. It was Founded in 1988.

Friends of Earth is an international membership organisation, with members spread across the world. Its advocacy programs focus on environmental issues, highlighting their social, political and human rights contexts.

The World Wide Fund for Nature Inc. (WWF) is an international non-governmental organization founded in 1961 that works in the field of wilderness preservation and the reduction of human impact on the environment.

Wildlife Conservation Society is a non-governmental organization headquartered at the Bronx Zoo in New York City, that aims to conserve the world's largest wild places in 14 priority regions. It was founded in 1895 as the New York Zoological Society (NYZS).

John Muir Trust The John Muir Trust is a community focused conservation charity dedicated to the experience, protection and repair of wild places across the UK. It was founded in 1983. Their purpose is to experience, protect and restore wild places.

National Geographic Society or NGS, headquartered in Washington, D.C., United States, is one of the largest non-profit scientific and educational organizations in the world. The Society operates the magazine, TV channels, a website, worldwide events.

BirdLife international has as its primary mission to conserve birds, their habitats and global biodiversity, working with people toward sustainability in the use of natural resources. They are a global family of over 115 national Partners.

Ocean Care is committed to marine wildlife protection. Since 2011 with Special Consultative Status on marine issues with the Economic and Social Council of the United Nations.

Greenpeace is an independent global organization founded in 1971. Greenpeace states its goal is to ensure the ability of the Earth to nurture life in all its diversity.
